Soccer

I love playing soccer, which has been a big part of my life and taught me teamwork, discipline, and how to push through challenges. Hunting & Fishing

Hunting and fishing have also been a big part of who I am. My father grew up in the middle of the woods and learned from his grandfather the skills of trapping, hunting, and fishing for food. I was raised to be a hunter with my father, growing up in the middle of nowhere Minnesota where we lived off the land. Watching him pass down these techniques and traditions has taught me patience, respect for nature, and the importance of learning from those who came before you.
