Why I Chose University of Minnesota Duluth

The reason I came to UMD was made by a variety of reasons. My dad attended UMD and had an absolute blast. I still hear his stories about him and his friends and all the good times he had. And just those stories made it that much more interesting for me.

I also spent a lot of time with my dad as a kid and picked up a lot of his traits. These traits were part of his business traits. So exploring classes within the business field became an interest of mine at UMD.

Next was the pure location of UMD. I am from Hudson, which is 2:30 hours away from Duluth but I also have lots of family in International Falls, which is also 2:30 hours away making UMD a perfect place to be able to travel to either location.

Here in Duluth there is also Spirit Mountain the ski resort which is an amazing way to pass time with these cold long winters.

What I Love About Duluth

Duluth, Minnesota, offers a unique blend of natural beauty, outdoor recreation, and vibrant community life that makes it an exceptional place to live and study. The city's location on the shores of Lake Superior provides stunning views and countless opportunities for outdoor activities year-round.

The area's natural landscape is breathtaking, with the lake, forests, and nearby state parks offering endless opportunities for hiking, fishing, and exploring. The changing seasons bring their own beauty, from colorful fall foliage to pristine winter snowscapes.

Duluth's community is welcoming and tight-knit, with a strong sense of local pride. The city offers a great balance between small-town charm and urban amenities, with excellent restaurants, local shops, and cultural events throughout the year.

The proximity to outdoor recreation opportunities, combined with the academic excellence of UMD, creates an ideal environment for both personal growth and professional development.

Personal Experiences & Campus Highlights

My time at UMD has been transformative, both academically and personally. The business program has provided me with a strong foundation in sales, marketing, and relationship building that aligns perfectly with my career goals.

The campus community is supportive and engaging, with professors who are genuinely invested in student success. The smaller class sizes allow for meaningful interactions and personalized learning experiences.

Living in Duluth has also allowed me to maintain close connections with family while pursuing my education. The balance between academic rigor and the natural beauty of the area creates an inspiring environment for growth and learning.

Whether it's studying in the library with views of Lake Superior, participating in campus events, or taking advantage of the nearby outdoor recreation, UMD offers a well-rounded college experience that extends far beyond the classroom.
